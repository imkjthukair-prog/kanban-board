Every Sunday, I review all feedback from approved/rejected suggestions and update my understanding of what works for Khaled.

## Current Patterns (Updated Sunday, March 1, 2026)

### What Gets Approved ✓
- Ideas that **fit current momentum/format** (e.g., quote reactions approved even when modified suggestion)
- **Strategic investor credibility moves** with tight timelines (e.g., LinkedIn investor credibility post for app deadline)
- Suggestions that **leverage existing assets** without requiring new execution styles
- Ideas with **clear value alignment** to immediate business priorities

### What Gets Rejected ✗
- **Redundant suggestions** (LinkedIn already active — he knows the platforms)
- Tasks requiring **different filming/execution format** when he's in a momentum phase with current format
- Suggestions that **don't prioritize timing** (funnel audit during Ramadan = deprioritized until post-Ramadan)
- Ideas that **already exist** (investment pitch already done)
- Attempts to **impose rigid frameworks** in relationship-driven markets (fixed pricing sheet won't work for Saudi negotiation culture)
- Casual pilots with unfamiliar contacts (prefers warm relationships + established credibility)

### Timing Insights ⏰
- **During Ramadan/Eid:** Marketing/funnel work deprioritized. Focus is operations + strategy.
- **Application deadlines:** He has urgent bandwidth for investor credibility/positioning
- **Format discovery phase:** When he finds a winning content format, he doubles down until saturation — don't interrupt with format changes
- **Suggest post-Ramadan work 3 weeks ahead** so he's mentally prepped

### Strategic Priorities 🎯
- **Saudi Speakers is the scaling business right now** (4 programs in 4 cities simultaneously = operational priority)
- **Personal brand momentum is high** (quote-reaction format working — feed this)
- **College of Life is in sustainable mode** (post-Ramadan focus, not urgent now)
- **Relationship/warmth > process/frameworks** (he values knowing contacts, not cold pilots)
- **Investor positioning is live** (ظهور deal suggests capital conversations happening)

## Suggestion Guidelines (Evolved from Feedback)

Based on feedback patterns, I should:

1. **Prioritize** suggestions that:
   - Bridge his existing momentum (e.g., quote reactions → College of Life success stories)
   - Scale bottleneck operations (4 programs = needs ops support)
   - Leverage his biggest asset (1.5M followers as direct revenue/engagement driver)
   - Align with investor visibility phase (what he told his investors he'd do?)

2. **Avoid** suggestions that:
   - Require format changes during momentum phases
   - Suggest redundant platforms/ideas (he already knows his landscape)
   - Ignore timing (Ramadan = different priorities)
   - Impose process over relationships/negotiation
   - Assume he hasn't thought of obvious things (always assume he has, just blocked by priority/timing)

3. **Time** suggestions when:
   - 3 weeks before a season change (post-Ramadan, post-Eid)
   - After hearing he's stuck on something specific (quote-finding friction → suggest library system)
   - When new business constraints appear (4 programs = ops manager suggestion)
   - During visible momentum phases (investor visibility = investor credibility ideas)
